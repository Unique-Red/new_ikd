Meet Our Executive Chairman


Honourable Wasiu Adesina

Honourable Wasiu Adesina is the Executive Chairman, Ikorodu local government. He is a man loved by his people and widely acknowledged as a performer in the bracket of Executive Chairmen in Lagos State having won the best performing local government Chairman award in Lagos at far away Abuja, Nigeria.
Honourable Wasiu Adesina was born in Ikorodu over 55 years ago to the family of Pa Karimu Adesina. He attended Ijomu Primary School, Ikorodu and Oriwu College for his secondary education. Upon the completion of his secondary education, he joined the Civil Service in 1983 where he worked with the Ministry of Local Government and Community development.
His early love for social work necessitated his enrolling for community development program, which led him to obtain a certificate in Community development and Adult education in 1986 before proceeding to the University of Ibadan for a Diploma in Community development service.
WADESH ventured into politics in 1989 under the platform of the Social Democratic Party. He became a Supervisory Councilor at the Ikorodu Local Government in 1991. The zeal for higher education led Honourable Adesina to pursue his first degree in Political Science in 1998 from the Lagos state University and his Masters degree in 2001 from the same institution.
Honourable Adesina is a Member of the Nigerian Institute of Management and a Certified Public Administrator. He is an International Consultant and he consults for United Nations Development Program (UNDP) and other international Agencies on development strategies and issues. He was involved in the development of Bauchi State economic empowerment development strategies also Youth development in the Niger Delta Zone which includes Rivers, Delta and Bayelsa states.
He has won numerous awards such as the best performing Chairman in Nigeria, Outstanding Performance award, humanitarian service award and many others. He is married to Mrs Abolanle Adesina and the marriage is blessed with wonderful children.
